Location

Its prime location is at Nemmara. This is the first self financing Arts & Science College in Nemmara Block Panchayath. It is easily accessible for students from nearest panchayath namely – Aylur, Nemmara, Melarcode, Nelliyampathy, Elavancherry and Pallasena and also the midway and of vicinity of Pollachi – Thrissur state Highway. The college shall be committed to develop as a value and need based quality education provider in our area.
